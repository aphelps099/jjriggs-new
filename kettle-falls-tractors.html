Who We Are

JJ Riggs Equipment opened in 2019 to serve landowners in Stevens County — including Kettle Falls and the surrounding areas along the Columbia River. We're a family operation, not a franchise. When you call, you talk to someone who knows this area and the equipment that works here.

What We Do

We're the authorized TYM tractor dealer for northeastern Washington. New tractors, used equipment, parts, service, and warranty repairs. Whether you're maintaining land near Lake Roosevelt or working acreage out toward Northport, we've got you covered.
