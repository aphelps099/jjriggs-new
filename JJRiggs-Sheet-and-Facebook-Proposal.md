One Google Sheet becomes the single place your team manages inventory. Every tractor, mower, and implement on the website is a row in that sheet — name, model, brand, specs, price, photo, and description.

- **Mark what's on the lot.** A Status dropdown on every row: *On the Lot · Low Stock · On Order · Out of Stock*. **Built so it can't be messed up:**
