Financing questions

What credit do I need to qualify?

A range of credit profiles can qualify, and approvals depend on the lender's review. The fastest way to find out is the no-obligation online application — it won't commit you to anything.

What do I need to apply?

Basic personal and income information, plus the equipment you're interested in. The online application walks you through each step in just a few minutes.

What terms are available?

Terms commonly range from 24 to 84 months depending on the model and current programs. Promotional rates like 0% and 0.99% APR vary by model and current program — ask us what's available right now.

Can I put money down or trade in?

Yes. A down payment lowers your monthly payment, and we accept trade-ins toward your purchase. Bring your trade by the lot or tell us about it when you request a quote.

Do you finance implements and attachments too?

Often, yes — implements bought with a tractor can usually be included. Ask us and we'll bundle it into your quote.
